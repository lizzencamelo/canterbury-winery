Better wine for all

Canterbury wines are made in small batches in the cathedral town of Canterbury, Kent.

Sustainably Farmed

We source fruit from sustainably farmed vineyards throughout Northern Kent, England.

Made By Hand

Our wines are made by hand in small batches using minimal vinification techniques.

Shipped Directly To You

We sell our wine direct to help keep prices as low as possible.

The Estate
